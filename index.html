Places You Must Visit

prithvi cafe

i had visited in dec 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.



jantar mantar jaipur

i had visited in feb 2019

It is a wonderful place. Must visit this place. Hiring a tour guide will be better to understand the stories of such a historical place. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls. It is a historical site and famous among tourists. It contains a number of different devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the Indian history. It has a nominal ticket price of ₹15 for Indians but about ₹225-250 for foreigners. Nice place to visit. We had an incredible experience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of the astronomical instruments built around 300 years ago.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

"Avengers: Endgame" is a cinematic triumph that seamlessly weaves together a decade of storytelling, delivering an emotional and action-packed conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. The film excels in its balance of intense battles and heartfelt character moments, with standout performances from the ensemble cast. As it pays homage to its own legacy and introduces new dynamics, "Endgame" stands as a testament to the power of interconnected storytelling, leaving fans both satisfied and nostalgic for the remarkable journey that brought Earth's mightiest heroes together.

The Lion King
Nice for 1 time view

"The Lion King (2019) roars onto the screen with a visual grandeur that is nothing short of breathtaking. Jon Favreau's live-action adaptation seamlessly combines cutting-edge technology with the timeless narrative of Simba's journey, delivering a visually stunning experience. The realistic animation captures the essence of the iconic characters, while the stellar voice cast, led by Donald Glover and Beyoncé, breathes fresh life into the classic tale. Nostalgia reigns supreme as the film pays homage to the 1994 animated masterpiece, and the enduring themes of responsibility and the circle of life resonate with a new generation. Despite its familiarity, 'The Lion King' stands as a majestic cinematic achievement that captivates both the heart and the senses.

Sonic the Hedgehog
Sonic the Hedgehog movie races into theaters with a delightful blend of nostalgia, humor, and visually dynamic action, successfully bringing the iconic blue speedster to life for fans of all ages.

"Sonic the Hedgehog" races onto the big screen with a delightful blend of nostalgia and modern charm, offering a lively and entertaining adventure for audiences of all ages. The film successfully reimagines the iconic blue speedster with a winning performance by Ben Schwartz, who brings Sonic's irreverent personality to life. Jim Carrey's portrayal of the villainous Dr. Robotnik adds a touch of classic Carrey humor to the mix. The storyline, while straightforward, is engaging, and the film strikes a commendable balance between heartfelt moments and high-speed action sequences. Visually dynamic and filled with nods to the beloved video game, "Sonic the Hedgehog" proves to be a fun and satisfying cinematic journey for both fans and newcomers alike.
